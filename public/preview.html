Key Findings

Six things we learned about how YouTube campaigns work

The Multiformat Rollout

What content surrounds the hero video — and how much of it is planned?

YouTube recommends a multiformat rollout around every release. Does the hero get a Lyric Video, a Visualiser, or both? Is anything published in the 7–14 day follow-up window? The content built around the hero is what turns a single upload into a campaign.
